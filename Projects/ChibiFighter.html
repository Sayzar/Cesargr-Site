Chibi Fighter

Chibi Fighter was a game that was developed during my second year at UCI and my first year as a Computer Science major. The game was a local multiplayer game. It featured 3 different stages in which players can fight and numerous items the players can use granting them different abilities. The game was tile-based and programmed in Java.
